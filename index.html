Our Story

About the Academy

Passionately preserving the classical tradition of Odissi while inspiring students to experience the spiritual and artistic soul of Odisha.

Welcome to Nrutyapuri Dance Academy, where the enchanting world of Odissi dance comes alive. Located in the heart of Hyderabad, we are a premier dance school dedicated to preserving and promoting the rich cultural heritage of Odissi, offering comprehensive training for students of all ages and skill levels.

We believe dance is not just a physical activity but a powerful means of self-expression and personal growth. Through classes, workshops, and performances, we instill pride, discipline, and cultural awareness in our students.

From your first step to your finest performance, our structured curriculum helps you master the fluid movements, expressive mudras, and soulful storytelling that make Odissi truly enchanting.
